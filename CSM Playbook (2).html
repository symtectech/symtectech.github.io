5 Customer Complaints Management

‚ñº
üìù Documentation Location
All complaints are documented in the Client Success MS Planner

üîÑ Complaint Resolution Process

  1. Document: Log complaint in Client Success MS Planner
  2. Assess Severity: Determine if partner involvement is needed immediately
  3. Follow Up: Contact both the person who submitted the complaint AND the technician who helped them
  4. Remediate: Take necessary corrective action
  5. Support Technician: Ensure technician is okay and provide constructive feedback
  6. Report: Inform a Partner of the situation and resolution
